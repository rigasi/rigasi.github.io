Unit 12


Unit 12 was the least challenging of the units as I am a professional project manager and therefore I could quickly browse through the content for this unit. This is an advantage as I know the tools and methods which can help me deliver the project but it is also a good reminder to utilise PM principles in the upcoming project. They often say that Doctors are the worst when it comes to curing themselves and I would say this holds true for project managers as well. We might be good at running projects with others and facilitating their work through servant leadership but sometimes we are not good at managing our own projects and ourselves. What I keep from this unit is that I should be proactive in fighting my own complacency and to apply project management principles in my own project!
