Unit 12


In unit 12 the main debate was on what will be the most influential trend in SRM in the years to come. As discussed in unit 11 , Group 5 was of the opinion that the most interesting trend for the future SRM would me a combination of Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. Naturally as the two fields are too wide we had to focus our efforts on one of the two and therefore we picked Machine Learning as our lucky candidate. It was interesting to see that some other groups also chose ML/AI combinations and as expected this combination was the winning candidate in the voting process.
