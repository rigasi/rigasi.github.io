Unit 3


In unit 3 different approached to threat modelling were introduced. I was mostly aware of STRIDE and DREAD but I have never used attack trees or PASTA.

The Threat Modeling Manifesto (Shostack et al, 2020) was an interesting adaptation of the agile manifesto in risk management processes whereas the paper by Shevchenko et al (2018) offered a very interesting summary of threat modelling methods which can be used in different parts of development processes. The threat modelling tool by microsoft is a free platform which can be used for carrying out threat modelling activities.
